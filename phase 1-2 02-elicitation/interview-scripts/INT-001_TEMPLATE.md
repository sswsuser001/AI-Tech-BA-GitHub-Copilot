## Opening (5 min)

**Objective:** Build rapport, explain purpose, set expectations.

- Thank you for making time
- Purpose: Understand [stakeholder perspective on key topic]
- Duration: ~60 minutes
- Questions will be layered—comfortable to say "I don't know"
- No right/wrong answers; we're exploring

---

## Section 1: [Topic] (15 min)

**Context Question (Open):**
- What is your current understanding of [topic]?

**Probe Questions (Layered):**
1. When you say "[phrase they used]", what do you mean specifically?
2. How does that affect your day-to-day work?
3. What would ideal look like?

---

## Section 2: [Topic] (15 min)

**Context Question (Open):**
- Walk me through how [process] works today.

**Probe Questions (Layered):**
1. Who else is involved in that decision?
2. What's the biggest pain point there?
3. If you could change one thing, what would it be?

---

## Section 3: Priorities (10 min)

**Priority Question (Ranking):**
- Of these three areas [A/B/C], which matters most to you and why?

**Consequence Question:**
- If we could only fix one, which would have the biggest impact?

---

## Closing (5 min)

**Wrap-up:**
- What have I missed or misunderstood?
- Who else should we talk to?
- Any final thoughts?
