## Layer 1: Discovery (Open, Non-Leading)

These questions have no expected answer. Listen for the stakeholder's own framing.

| # | Question | Expected Answer Type | Why This Matters |
|---|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| 1 | What does [term] mean to you? | Free narrative | Baseline understanding |
| 2 | Walk me through how [process] works today. | Step-by-step flow | Current state clarity |
| 3 | What's working well about [area]? | Positive aspects | Preserve what works |
| 4 | What's not working about [area]? | Pain points | Identify problems |

---

## Layer 2: Probe (Specific, Clarifying)

Follow up on Layer 1 answers. Don't ask these first.

| # | Question | Probe If | Why This Matters |
|---|----------|----------|-----------------|
| 1 | When you said "[phrase]", what did you mean specifically? | They used vague language | Reduce ambiguity |
| 2 | Who else is involved in that decision? | They said "we decide" | Map stakeholders |
| 3 | How often does [scenario] happen? | They mentioned a problem | Quantify frequency |
| 4 | What's the impact when [scenario] happens? | They described an issue | Quantify severity |

---

## Layer 3: Rank (Prioritization, Trade-off)

Only ask after Layers 1 & 2 establish context.

| # | Question | Purpose | Why This Matters |
|---|----------|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | Of these three areas [A/B/C], which matters most to you and why? | Forced ranking | Uncover priority |
| 2 | If we could only fix one, which would have the biggest impact? | Trade-off scenario | Identify critical path |
| 3 | What's the cost of not fixing [issue]? | Consequence | Justify investment |

---

## Scoring Rubric

After the interview, score clarity on this uncertainty:

| Clarity Score | Definition | Action |
|---------------|-----------|--------|
| 1–3 | Completely unclear; conflicting views | Schedule follow-up |
| 4–6 | Somewhat clear; some open questions | Add to Tier 2 uncertainties |
| 7–8 | Clear; minor clarifications may be needed | Move to resolved, flag for glossary |
| 9–10 | Crystal clear; ready to document | Document in BRD; no follow-up needed |
